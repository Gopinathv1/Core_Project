Imagine you are working for Samsung Electronics.

The company has 40 manufacturing machines producing electronic components 24×7.

Currently:

Engineers inspect machines manually.
Defects are identified only after production.
Machine failures cause downtime.
Maintenance is scheduled manually.
Managers lack a unified dashboard.

The company wants an AI platform that can answer:

Which machines are likely to fail?
Which products are likely to be defective?
What factors cause defects?
Which shifts perform poorly?
Which operators need additional training?
